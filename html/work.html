What are my areas of expertise?

Versatility is what I describe myself. I had time working on a wide variety of research or commercial projects, respectively hardware, software, and web development. FPGA-based hardware design, embedded systems development, desktop application build, and full-stack web application deployment are areas I've participated in. My areas of expertise might be broader as I'll delve into the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for years to come.

Vin Decode

Nov 2013 - Jan 2014

Every single car has its own Vehicle Identification Number, which associates with its specific "Year", "Make", "Model", and "Engine". This project served as a feature in Car Diagnostic Tools, which helped decode VIN code into human-friendly information.

Network Scan

Dec 2013 - Jun 2014

This project helped parse the Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) of a specific vehicle. This feature had two modes, respectively automatic scan and manual scan. While the first feature automatically scanned all trouble codes in all car modules, the latter enabled users to manual select a specific module to scan.

Battery Reset

Nov 2013 - Mar 2014

My work involves building databases, retrieving data from databases, and displaying essential information which guides users through the steps of resetting the car battery.

DLC Locator

Nov 2013 - Jan 2014

Every automotive is equipped with a DLC Connector which serves as a gate for communicating with external devices; however, its location varies across different models and brands. The project aimed to solve this problem by building a user interface and providing users with essential information, which helped identify the exact location of a DLC port easier.

Oil Reset

Oct 2013 - Dec 2013

To maintain car operation, changing the oil and resetting the oil system should be done regularly. The steps for such a process are not always clear and similar across different cars, hence the need for the Oil Reset feature. The project targeted displaying "Oil Reset" steps in car diagnostic tools, which reduced the challenges posed by distinct vehicle brands.

DTC Definition

Oct 2013 - Dec 2013

A basic but indispensable feature in Automotive Electronic Control Modules is to send error codes to diagnostic devices; however, none of them are user-understandable. My work involved converting trouble codes into their corresponding definitions and displaying them on the diagnostic tools.
